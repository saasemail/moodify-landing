Moodify Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Moodify collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Data Collection

Moodify does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All image processing is done locally on your device.

2. Camera Access

The app requests camera permission only to capture selfies for generating humorous content. Images never leave your phone.

3. No Analytics

We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking tools. Moodify is designed to be privacy-friendly from the ground up.

4. Third-party Sharing

No data is shared with any third parties. Ever.

5. Policy Updates

If we ever decide to update this policy, changes will be posted here before they take effect.
